§ Apple Pie

Recipe taken from Taste of Home recipes site

Kid or Adult, nothing compares to a good Apple Pie.
Hungry or Not, Happy or Sad, it's Always the moment to Have One!

Let's see below step-by-step how to prepare this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

dough for double-crust pie 1pcs.
sugar white 1/3 cup
brown 1/3 cup
all-purpose flour 1/4 cup
condiments ground cinamon 1 teaspoon
ground ginger 1/4 teaspoon
ground nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon
thinly sliced peeled tart apples 6-7 cups
lemon juice 1 tablespoon
butter 1 tablespoon
egg white 1 large
Optional: Turbinado or coarse sugar, ground cinnamon, vanilla bean ice cream and caramel sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°. On a lightly floured surface, roll half of the dough to a 1/8-in.-thick circle; transfer to a 9-in. pie plate. Chill while preparing filling. In a small bowl, combine sugars, flour and spices. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat. Add filling; dot with butter.
  2. Roll remaining dough to a 1/8-in.-thick circle. Place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edge. Cut slits in top. Beat egg white until foamy; brush over crust. If desired, sprinkle with turbinado sugar and ground cinnamon.
  3. Bake on the lowest rack 60-70 minutes, until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, covering with foil halfway if crust begins to get too dark. Cool on a wire rack. If desired, serve with ice cream and caramel sauce.
  4. Bon Apetit!

§ Tartufo

Recipe taken from Taste of Home recipes site

Child or Adult, who doesn't like sweets.
Are you ready to discover a Very Delicious Recipe?

This dome-shaped dessert hails from Pizzo, Calabria, in Southern Italy. It's made with ice cream, filled with cookies and enrobed in rich dark chocolate.

What Does Tartufo Mean in Italian?

Tartufo is the Italian word for “truffle.” The expensive mushroom foraged in the Italian countryside might come to mind, but this word also refers to a delicious ice cream confection made with layers of ice cream, chocolate, cherries and cookie crumbs with a chocolate shell finish.
Cracking into the hard chocolate shell is the best part as it uncovers the riches inside!

Let's see below step-by-step how to prepare this sweet bomb.
This recipe makes one Tartufo that serves two people.

Ingredients

chocolate ice crea, softened 1 cup
Amarena cherries 1 cup
jarred hot fudge 1 tablespoon
Amaretti Cookies, crushed 12 pcs.
vanilla ice cream, softened 1 cup
topping dark chocolate chips 1 cup
virgin coconut oil 3 tablespoons
crushed hazelnuts 1-4 pcs.

Directions

  1. Press the ice cream into a bowl
    1. Place a 5-inch glass bowl on the counter and line with a large piece of cling wrap. You want at least 3 inches of overhang.
    2. Scoop the chocolate ice cream into the bowl and spread so the top is flat.
    3. Editor’s Tip: Move the ice cream from the freezer to the refrigerator to soften about 20 minutes before using.
  2. Add cherries, fudge and cookies
    1. Place the cherries on top and press into the chocolate ice cream.
    2. Spoon the fudge on top, then add 2 tablespoons of the crushed cookies.
    3. Spread and flatten up to the sides of the bowl.
  3. Add another layer of ice cream
    1. Add the vanilla ice cream and spread in an even layer. Press the rest of the cookie crumbs into the vanilla ice cream to create a cookie base.
    2. Fold the cling wrap up over the top of the tartufo and place the bowl in the freezer overnight.
  4. Make the topping
    1. The next day, make the chocolate shell topping. Melt the chocolate with coconut oil, then mix to combine.
  5. Plate the tartufo
    1. Remove the bowl from the freezer and carefully remove the molded ice cream from the bowl. First, unwrap the cling wrap and place a small piece of parchment on top of the bowl.
    2. Flip the molded ice cream onto a wire rack on top of a baking sheet. You may need a butter knife to help the ice cream release.
    3. Carefully remove the cling wrap and pour the chocolate sauce all over the tartufo. It will harden within a minute so quickly cover with crushed nuts and place it back in the freezer until ready to eat!
    4. Bon Apetit!

§ Baklava

Recipe taken from Taste of Home recipes site

We're all children when it comes to Turkish sweets.
Ready to discover them?

The origin of Baklava is unknown and the same can be said in regard to the ethymology of the word.

Let's see below step-by-step how to prepare this traditional Turkish dish.

Ingredients

sugar 1cup
large eggs, room temperature 2 pcs.
almond extract 1 teaspoon
vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
all-purpose flour 2-1/4 cups
baking powder 1 teaspoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
topping sugar 3 tablespoons
ground cinnamon 2 teaspoons
honey 1/2 cup
chopped walnuts 3/4 cup

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, cream sugar and butter until blended. Beat in eggs, 1 at a time, and extracts. In another bowl, whisk flour, baking powder and salt; gradually beat into creamed mixture. Wrap dough; refrigerate until firm enough to form into balls, about 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°. For topping, combine sugar and cinnamon; set aside. Shape dough into 1-in. balls; refrigerate again if dough becomes too warm. Place balls 2-1/2 in. apart on parchment-lined baking sheets. Bake 8 minutes. Press a deep indentation in center of each cookie with the back of a rounded teaspoon. Fill each indentation with honey and walnuts; sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Return to oven and bake until edges begin to brown, 7-9 minutes longer. Cool on pans 1 minute. Remove to wire racks to cool. Store in an airtight container.
  3. Bon Apetit!
